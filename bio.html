Bio

Eleanor is a filmmaker based in New York.
She works as a producer and director across film, television, digital, and branded projects. In 2021, she was nominated for a Daytime Emmy for producing the feature-length documentary Call Your Mother. She has produced films that have screened at Tribeca Film Festival and SXSW and nonfiction TV series for networks including National Geographic, PBS, AMC, Disney+, Netflix, Hulu, SundanceTV, and Viceland.
